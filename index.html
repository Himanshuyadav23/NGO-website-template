About IYWS

Vision, mission & the spark that started it all

Vision

To build a socially responsible and empowered youth community that actively contributes to nation-building and positive societal change.

Mission

  • Connect youth with opportunities in social impact, skill development, and leadership
  • Collaborate with colleges, corporates, and communities for real-world change
  • Create platforms where young people can learn, lead, and contribute

Why we started

Today's youth has immense potential but often lacks direction and the right opportunities. We saw a gap between students, corporates, and social impact — and built IYWS to bridge it.

Youth empowerment Collaboration Impact-driven Integrity Growth mindset

When youth is guided in the right direction, they can transform not just their own lives, but society as a whole.

Pranjal Yadav Founder, IYWS
